INTI Shuttle Bus

Reasons for Choosing US

The college shuttle bus service offers students a more comfortable and convenient way to arrive at our school, providing a hassle-free transportation solution that eliminates the stress of driving in traffic, searching for parking, and dealing with the costs of gas and maintenance associated with personal vehicles.


 Students, faculty, staff and visitors to campus can hop off at the nearest public transportation stop and catch a free shuttle service  within the city where the university is located. This free shuttle will depart from  the school at 7am, 9am and 11am. The bus  leaves the school at 12.20pm, 2.20pm, 3.20pm, 4.20pm and 6.10pm respectively
